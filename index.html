Fleshlight Launch Experimental Controller v0.1

Needs Chrome 56+ on Mac/ChromeOS/Android M (6.0+)/Linux (If on linux, turn on Experimental Web Extensions)

S = Speed of stroke
D = Distance of stroke
O = Offset (position to start from, the down stroke moves to this)
i = interval (time between strokes)

Use at your own risk, this is unsupported and experimetal, you could hurt yourself if not careful.

I hacked this abomination together from raunch-js, because the buttons on the Launch are retarded.
It turns out that having more control is awesome.

What does this do exactly?:
Every interval (i, seconds), sends an up stroke of distance (d, %) at speed (s, %) to the launch, and after interval (i) returns to offest (o) at speed (s).

I limited the interval to 150ms because the Launch crashes with lower than that.
